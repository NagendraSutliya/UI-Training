The Boozy Apple Shake Recipe

Description

An apple a day keeps the doctor away, but this shake is a pure punch of crunchy apples, almond milk and Bailey’s topped with some whipped cream to give this drink an exotic touch. This drink is perfect for parties, when you want to enjoy a subtle high with a fruity and creamy punch. Made like a simple shake but punched with Bailey’s gives this drink a heavenly taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup apple
  • 60 ml baileys irish cream
  • whipped cream as required
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 2 Pinches cinnamon
  • ice cubes as required

Steps

  1. Step 1 Wash the apples:
    2. To begin with this easy recipe, wash and cut the apples.
  2. Step 2 Blend it all:
    3. In a blender, add in the apples, almond milk and ice cubes, blend into a thick shake.
  3. Step 3 Pour the drink:
    4. Pour Irish cream and blend again. Pour into the glasses and add whipped cream.
  4. Step 4 Enjoy:
    5. Sprinkle with cinnamon powder and enjoy chilled!
